What are online classes?

Online course materials for certain classes may be purchased if you wish to study at home or at your own pace.
These materials are available throughout the year, and can be purchased regardless of the current semester or class schedule.
Purchasing an onine class will give you access to the full curriculum, without the hands-on teaching.

Each online class is listed below, along with a short description of what it consists of.
