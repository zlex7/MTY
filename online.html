What are online classes?

Online course materials for certain classes may be purchased if you wish to study at home or at your own pace.
These materials are available throughout the year, and can be purchased regardless of the current semester or class schedule.
Purchasing an onine class will give you access to the full curriculum, without the hands-on teaching.

Each online class is listed below, along with a short description of what it consists of.

Online classes we offer

Fall 05 Pre-Algebra

This is the first semester of the yearly course, 05 Pre-Algebra. Its prerequisite is the Summer 05 Pre-Algebra course.

Topics covered in this class include:
• The Greatest Common Factor (GCF) of Two Integers
• The Least Common Multiple (LCM) of Two Integers
• Three Methods for Finding the GCF and LCM
• Linear Inequalities
• Systems of Linear Inequalities
• Applications of Linear Equations and Inequalities

Fall 06 Algebra

This is the first semester of the yearly course, 06 Algebra. Its prerequisite is the course, Spring 05 Pre-Algebra.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Review of Distance and Midpoint Formulas
• Slope of a Line
• Linear Equations in Two Variables
• Standard, Point-Slope, and Slope-Intercept Form
• Systems of Linear Equations
• Substitution and Elimination Methods
• Applications of Systems of Linear Equations

Summer 06 Geometry

This is the first Geometry course offered by MTY Academy. Its prerequisite is the Spring 05 Algebra course.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Line Segments, Parallel and Perpendicular Lines, and Transversals
• Line Segment Bisectors and Angle Bisectors
• Angles (Vertical, Corresponding, Alternate Interior/Exterior, Complementary, Supplementary)
• Triangles and Their Properties
• Three Methods for Finding the Area of a Triangle
• Special Triangles (Isosceles, Equilateral, Right)
• Polygons and their Properties
• Circles and Their Properties

Summer 07 Geometry

This is the second Geometry course offered by MTY Academy. This course emphasizes logical thinking and proofs. Its prerequisites are the Summer 06 Geometry and Spring 06 Algebra courses.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Logic and Proofs
• Introduction to Two-Column Proofs
• Parallel Lines and Polygons
• Congruence and Similarity of Polygons

Fall 07 Algebra

This is the first semester of the yearly course, 07 Algebra. Its prerequisite is the Spring 06 Algebra course.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Review of Factoring Polynomials
• Solving Quadratic Equations
• Properties of Solutions of Quadratic Equations
• Quadratic Type Equations
• Polynomial Equations
• Applications of Quadratic Equations
• Solving Polynomial Inequalities
• Polynomial Division (Long and Synthetic)

Spring 07 Algebra

This is the second semester of the yearly course, 07 Algebra. Its prerequisite is the Fall 07 Algebra course.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Rational Expressions and Their Applications
• Complex Rational Expresions
• Solving Equations Involving Rational Expressions
• Radicals and Radical Rules
• Simplifying Expressions Involving Radicals
• Solving Equations Involving Radicals
• Rational Exponents and Rules
• Simplifying Expressions Involving Rational Exponents
• Introduction to Complex Numbers

This is the first semester of the yearly course, 08 Algebra. Its prerequisite is the Spring 07 Algebra course.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Complex Numbers and Their Properties
• The Fundamental Theorem of Algebra
• The Remainder Theorem
• The Factor Theorem
• The Rational Root Theorem
• Sketching Graphs of Polynomial Functions

Summer 08 Geometry

This is the third Geometry course offered by MTY Academy. This course is designed to study geometry using coordinate systems. Its prerequisites are the Summer 07 Geometry or Spring 07 Algebra courses.

Topics covered in this class include:
• Circles
• Ellipses
• Parabolas
• Hyperbolas
