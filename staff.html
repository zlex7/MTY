Dr. Wezien Gu

Founded by Dr. Gu, MTY Club started with a group of Mathematically Talented Youth taking math courses at the Austin Great Wall Chinese School taught by Dr. Diana Gu. To expand and be able to offer education to more children, Dr. Gu decided to start MTY Academy. The goal of MTY Academy is to serve as a resource for young students to succeed in learning mathematics.
