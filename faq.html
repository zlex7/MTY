Frequently asked questions

For in person assistance, contact us here.

Q: Why is the “My Account” tab not always visible?

A: The “My Account” tab is only visible after you sign in. It allows you to do user-specific tasks such as registering and paying for classes and changing your profile information.

Q: Why should I create an account at mtyacademy.com?

A: Class registration and all course materials are online. Information such as the classes available for registration, registration status, emails about classes, homework, progress reports, and other class related activities are only available to those with an account. An account is needed to register for classes or receive any course materials.

Q: How do I sign up as a member of mtyacademy.com?

A: To sign up as a member, you will need to provide a valid email address, a username, and a password with a minimum length of 6 characters (special characters are not allowed). A member account activation email will be sent to the email address provided. Follow the instructions on the email to activate your account. Other required contact information will be requested the first time you register for a class. If this contact information is not submitted (in other words, if you do not use the account to register for a class), the account is subject to deletion. MTY Academy only collects information required for you to take classes.

Q: What do I do if I forgot my username and/or password?

A: If you forget your username or password, you may retrieve them by clicking here and using the email address you registered with. If you do not remember your email address, or you have since changed your email address, please contact us here. Do NOT sign up for a new account, as you will have to re-register and pay for the class again to view class materials.

Q: How do I register for a class at MTY Academy?

A: To register for classes, you must first sign in to your account on the mtyacademy.com website. If you have not created an account yet, see how to sign up as a member. If you have not registered for a class before, you will be asked to provide the student’s information. If you are a parent and registering for your children, you must register each child with a separate account. Next, click on the “Register for Classes” tab and select the class you wish to register for. Please check carefully to see whether the class is right for you, and that the prerequisites are met. The registration form is NOT submitted until you click the submit button at the bottom of the page and a confirmation number is displayed. Unless you are taking a continuing class, the submitted registration is subject to approval. The amount of time it takes to gain approval varies depending on each class. If the registration is subject to approval, an email will be sent to you. Once you receive approval, you will be notified in an email. Finally, print the Payment Form and the Release Form, and make the requested payment. Your registration is complete when the requested payment is received and processed by MTY Academy.

If the class you wish to register for is full, you will have the option of adding yourself to the waiting list. If any seats reopen due to a lack of payment or unfinished registration, people on the waiting list will be added to the class and notified on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Q: When should I make the payment for a class?

A: Payments for a class can be made as soon as registration is approved. You can view your registration status as well as your payment status online. Your registration is complete only after the full payment requested has been received and processed by MTY Academy. Registration that has been approved but where payment has not been received is subject to be removed, especially in the case where there is a waiting list for the class. There will be a processing fee of $15 for payments made after class starts.

Q: What methods of payment are accepted by MTY Academy?

A: Cash, checks, and bank or cashier checks are accepted forms of payment. Cash must be delivered in person to our classroom location. Checks may be mailed to the address listed on the payment form. Electronic forms of payment such as eChecks and PayPal are also accepted.

Q: Are payments refundable?

A: Payments are refundable or partially refundable depending on the time a withdrawal email is received by MTY Academy. Apart from the first class, the refund amounts are 3/4, 1/2, 1/4, or none of the class fee paid if the withdraw request is received during the first, second, third, or last quarter of the class. Q: How much do I pay if I join a class after the class has already started?

A: The payment amounts are the full class fee, 3/4, 1/2, or 1/4 of the class fee, depending on whether you joined the class during the first, second, third, or final quarter of the class.

Q: What if I want to register two or more children for classes at MTY Academy?

A: If you are the parent of two or more children that would like to take classes at MTY Academy, you must open an account for each child. This ensures that student-specific materials such as grades and assignments can be sent to the correct person.

Q: Can I register for a class if I have not taken the prerequisite classes?

A: The teacher's consent is required to take a class without having taken the required prerequisites. In order to set up a meeting with the teacher, contact us here, or email the teacher of the class here.
