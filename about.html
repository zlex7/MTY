What is this?

MTY Academy offers enrichment classes to students from 5th grade to high school. The classes are designed to help them to excel in academic fields and stay more competitive among their peers. Students and parents alike have praised the classes for not only helping students achieve good grades in their schools, but also becoming fond of subjects. After taking classes at MTY Academy, many students have changed their attitude toward the subjects. They no longer think the subject (s) is something they are forced to deal with but rather an interesting subject to delve into.

The founder

Founded by Dr. Gu, MTY Club started with a group of Mathematically Talented Youth taking math courses at the Austin Great Wall Chinese School taught by Dr. Diana Gu. To expand and be able to offer education to more children, Dr. Gu decided to start MTY Academy. The goal of MTY Academy is to serve as a resource for young students to succeed in learning mathematics.

What we offer

The goal of MTY Academy is to provide an environment for youth to excel academically. At MTY Academy, teachers are not assigned to teach a course. Instead, our courses are offered by teachers who are experienced and love to teach the courses. All of our classes are held at 7801 North Lamar Blvd Suite F-38 Austin, TX 78752.
